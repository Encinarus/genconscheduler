About

Not much to say here. Gencon is a fantastic event but it's event listings and cart setup leave some to be desired. For the last two years my group has used gencon.highprogrammer.com (which is a huge step up from what gencon offers) but as our group grew, we wanted more. That's where this comes in.

Specifically, we wanted a way to track which events we're interested and share them with each other without going crazy for managing it. We only wanted a few things beyond what the highprogrammer site had:

  • Save events for later.
  • Sharing interested events.
  • Better search and clustering, specifically for figuring out what will fill free slots.

So, I made this. If you have any comments or suggestions, feel free to send them to gencon@lightpegasus.com
